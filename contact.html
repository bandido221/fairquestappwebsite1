Common Questions

How do I apply to a fair as a vendor or performer?

Go to the fair's listing on FairQuest, click Apply, and fill out the application form. You'll need a free FairQuest account first.

I'm a fair organizer — how do I get my fair on FairQuest?

Email us at fairquestapp@gmail.com or use the form above and select "I'm a fair organizer." We'll set everything up for you.

How long does it take to hear back on an application?

Application review times vary by fair. Once a fair approves or declines your application, you'll be notified through FairQuest.

Is FairQuest free to use?

Yes — FairQuest is free for vendors, performers, and attendees. Fair organizer tools are currently offered free during our founding partner program.
