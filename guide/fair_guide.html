PART 2 — GET YOUR FAIR ON FAIRQUEST

How to Add or Claim Your Fair

Getting your fair on FairQuest is a simple process — just send us an email and we'll take care of the rest.

1

Email Us

Send an email to fairquestapp@gmail.com and let us know one of the following:

• My fair is already on FairQuest — I'd like to claim ownership and manage it.
• My fair is not on FairQuest yet — I'd like to add it.

Include your fair name, your website or social media link, and the email address you used to create your FairQuest account.

💡 Don't have an account yet? Create one first using the User Guide above, then send us your email. We need your account to be set up before we can link you to your fair.
2

We Verify Your Ownership

Once we receive your email, we'll verify that you are the real owner or organizer of the fair. We do this by checking your fair's website, social media, and other public information.

💡 Tip: Make sure the email you contact us from matches your fair's official contact information — this helps us verify you quickly.
3

We Link Your Account to Your Fair

Once verified, we'll link your FairQuest account to your fair and grant you admin access. If your fair wasn't on FairQuest yet, we'll create the listing for you as well.

You'll receive a confirmation email once your access has been granted.

4

Access Your Fair Dashboard

Once your fair has been verified and approved, you'll receive a confirmation email. Log in to the same FairQuest account you used when you contacted us. On your profile page, a new Manage My Fairs button will appear. Click it to go to your Fair Dashboard.

If you manage more than one fair, you'll see all your verified fairs listed in the dashboard — each one accessible from the same place.

Once inside, you'll see all the fairs linked to your account. Click on the fair you want to manage to open that fair's dashboard and make any updates or changes.

💡 Tip: Make sure you log in with the same account you used when you contacted us — that's the account your fair access will be linked to.
PART 3 — YOUR FAIR DASHBOARD

Managing Your Fair

After your fair is created, your Fair Dashboard is where you manage everything. Here's what's available on the basic plan.

5

Access Your Fair Dashboard

From My Fairs, click on your fair to open the Fair Dashboard. This is your control center for managing your fair on FairQuest.

6

What You Can Do — Basic Plan

With a basic fair listing on FairQuest you can:

• Update Fair Info — edit your fair name, address, dates, hours, description, image, and contacts at any time.
• View Your Fair Page — see exactly what visitors see when they find your fair on FairQuest.
• My Fairs — manage all your fairs from one place if you run more than one event.

💡 Keep your dates up to date! Visitors and vendors rely on your weekend dates to plan ahead. Make sure they're always accurate.
