Getting Started as a Fair Owner

Everything you need to know to get your fair set up on FairQuest — from creating your account to managing vendors, applications, and payments.

PART 1 — CREATE YOUR ACCOUNT

Step 1: Create Your FairQuest Account

Just like vendors and performers, you start by creating a regular FairQuest account. This is how we identify who you are and link you to your fair.

1

Go to FairQuest & Sign Up

Visit fairquestapp.com and click Sign Up. Enter the email address you want to use for your fair admin account and choose a password.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the Sign Up / Create Account page
2

Verify Your Email

FairQuest sends a verification email to your address. Open it and click the verification link. You must verify before you can log in. Check spam if you don't see it.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the verification email from FairQuest
3

Log In & Go to Your Profile

After verifying, go back to fairquestapp.com and log in. Click Profile in the top menu.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the login page and main navigation
4

Complete Your Basic Profile

Fill in your profile information — this is important because FairQuest uses it to know who is making changes to your fair:

• Username — your display name
• First & Last Name — used for tracking who made changes
• City & State (optional)
• Profile Photo (optional)

Click Save & Continue.

📋 Why Your Name Matters

FairQuest logs every change made to your fair — who updated what and when. Your first and last name are used in this log. This helps keep your admin team accountable and makes it easy to track changes.

PART 2 — GET ACCESS TO YOUR FAIR

Step 2: Get Linked to Your Fair

Your fair needs to be added to FairQuest by the FairQuest team, and your account needs to be linked to it as an admin. Here's how that works.

1

Contact FairQuest to Add Your Fair

If your fair isn't on FairQuest yet, contact us at support@fairquestapp.com to get your fair added. We'll need your fair's name, location, and your account email.

🥇 Gold Tier Required for Full Features

Basic fair info is available to all fairs. To unlock online applications, vendor directory, and payment tracking, your fair needs to be a Gold tier fair. Contact us to learn more.

2

Receive Your Admin Access Email

Once your fair is set up, FairQuest will send you an email with a special access link. Click that link — it will connect your FairQuest account to your fair and give you admin access.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the admin access email with the link
3

Access Your Fair Admin Panel

After clicking the link, log in to FairQuest. You'll now see My Fairs in your profile menu. Click it to go to your Fair Admin panel — this is your control center for everything.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the My Fairs page in fair_admin.html
PART 3 — SET UP YOUR FAIR

Step 3: Set Up Your Fair

Now that you have access, here's what to do first to get your fair ready for vendors and performers to apply.

1

Update Your Fair Information

Click Update Fair on your dashboard. Fill in your fair's details — name, address, weekend dates, hours, description, and fair image. Make sure your weekend dates are correct — they power pricing and payment tracking.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the Update Fair page
2

Set Your Weekend Pricing

Go to Vendor Directory → Weekend Pricing. For each weekend, set the price for Artisan Vendors, Food Vendors, and the performer fee your fair will pay.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the Weekend Pricing page
3

Create Your Application Forms

Go to Applications → Manage Application Forms. Create one form for each type you want — Artisan Vendors, Food Vendors, Performers. Add your custom questions and paste your vendor contract.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the Manage Application Forms page
🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the form editor with custom questions
4

Tell Your Vendors & Performers to Apply

Now you're ready! Tell your vendors and performers to create a FairQuest account and apply. Send them to the vendor or performer guide:

🧺 Share Vendor Guide 🎭 Share Performer Guide

📢 What to tell them

"Create a free account at fairquestapp.com, find our fair, and click Apply. Your info will be filled in automatically — just answer the questions, pick your weekends, agree to the contract, and submit!"

PART 4 — MANAGING YOUR FAIR

Step 4: Day-to-Day Management

Once applications start coming in, here's how to manage everything from your dashboard.

1

Review & Approve Applications

Click Applications on your dashboard to see all submissions. Click Review Application on any row to see full details and approve or decline.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the Applications list page
2

Manage Your Vendor Directory

Click Vendor & Performer Directory to see your master list. Set invite status for next year, add notes, and see who is new vs returning. All changes are logged with your name automatically.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the Vendor Directory page
3

Track Payments

Click Payment Tracking to see all vendors and their payment status per weekend. When a payment comes in via PayPal, search the vendor by name or email, enter the amount and PayPal reference, and mark it paid.
