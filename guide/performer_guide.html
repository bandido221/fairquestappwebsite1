Getting Started as a Performer

Musicians, dancers, actors, combat performers, entertainers — here's how to set up your performer profile and apply to perform at Renaissance fairs on FairQuest.

PART 1 — CREATE YOUR ACCOUNT

First: Create a FairQuest Account

You need a FairQuest account before you can set up your performer profile. Same process as any other user.

1

Go to FairQuest & Sign Up

Visit fairquestapp.com and click Sign Up. Enter your email and choose a password.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the Sign Up page
2

Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for a verification email from FairQuest. Click the link to confirm your email address. Check spam if you don't see it.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the verification email
3

Log In & Complete Your Basic Profile

Log in, click Profile, and fill in your username, name, and optional photo. Click Save & Continue.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the profile setup page
PART 2 — SET UP YOUR PERFORMER PROFILE

Create Your Performer Profile

Your performer profile is your showcase — it's what fair owners see when deciding whether to book you.

1

Go to Your Profile & Click "Become a Performer"

From your profile page, find the option to set up a performer profile and click it.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the profile page showing the Become a Performer option
2

Fill In Your Performer Information

Add everything a fair owner needs to know about you:

• Performer / Act Name
• Description — what you do, your style, your experience
• Performer Type — musician, dancer, actor, combat, etc.
• Photos or Videos — show what your performance looks like
• Website or Social Links (optional)
• Contact Information

💡 Tip: A great description and strong photos make a big difference. Fair owners receive many applications — make yours stand out.
🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the performer profile creation form
3

Save Your Performer Profile

Click Save. Your profile is now active and will be used automatically on all your fair applications.

PART 3 — APPLY TO PERFORM AT A FAIR

Applying to Perform at a Fair

The application process for performers is similar to vendors, with a few differences — notably, the fair pays you, not the other way around.

1

Find a Fair Accepting Performer Applications

Browse fairs on FairQuest. Look for fairs with an Apply as Performer button. This means they are actively looking for performers through FairQuest.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of fair listing showing the Apply as Performer button
2

Click Apply — Your Info Is Pre-Filled

Click Apply. Your performer profile details — name, description, type, and contact info — are already filled in. No need to retype anything.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the performer application form with pre-filled info
3

Answer the Fair's Questions

The fair may ask specific questions — performance duration, space requirements, equipment needs, number of performers in your act, etc. Answer all required questions.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the custom performer questions
4

Select Which Weekends You're Available

Choose which weekends you can perform. You can select all weekends or just specific ones that work for your schedule.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the weekend picker for performers
5

Read & Accept the Performer Agreement

Read the fair's performer agreement — it covers performance expectations, schedule, conduct, and payment terms. Check the agreement box and submit.

🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the performer contract and agreement checkbox
6

Submit & Await Approval

Submit your application. The fair owner will review it and let you know if you're approved.

💰 How Performer Payment Works

Unlike vendors, performers are paid by the fair — not the other way around. Once approved and after performing, the fair will pay you the agreed fee per weekend. The fair tracks this in FairQuest's payment system.
