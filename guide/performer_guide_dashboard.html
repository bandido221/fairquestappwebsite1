PART 3 — YOUR DASHBOARD

Accessing Your Performer Dashboard

1

Find the Performer Dashboard Button

After submitting your application, a Performer Dashboard button will appear on your profile page. Click it to access your dashboard and check your application status at any time.

2

Your Dashboard Overview

The Performer Dashboard is your home base for managing your performer profile on FairQuest.

• My Profile — takes you back to your main FairQuest profile page.

• Add New Performer Account — allows you to add another performer profile if you have a second act under a different name.

• Update Profile — edit your performer name, bio, photos, contact info, and the fairs you're attending. See the Update Guide for details.

• Main Page — coming soon. This will allow performers to create a public-facing page with more details about their act.

• Delete — permanently removes your performer application. This cannot be undone.

• Fair Applications — apply to fairs that accept applications through FairQuest and check the status of your submissions.

💡 Your Fairs: Below your profile summary you'll see a list of the fairs you've applied to attend.
