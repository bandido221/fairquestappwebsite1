Getting Started as a User

Follow these steps to create your FairQuest account, verify your email, and set up your profile so you can explore fairs, save your favorites, and more.

STEP BY STEP

Creating Your Account

The whole process takes about 5 minutes. Here's exactly what to do.

1

Go to FairQuest & Click Sign Up

Visit fairquestapp.com and click Sign Up or Create Account. Enter your email address and choose a password.

2

Verify Your Email

After signing up, FairQuest sends a verification email to the address you used. Open that email and click the verification link. You must do this before you can log in.

💡 Tip: Check your spam or junk folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes.
🖼️ ADD IMAGE HERE Screenshot of the verification email showing the confirm button
3

Log In to Your Account

Once your email is verified, go back to fairquestapp.com and click Log In. Enter your email and password to access your account.

4

Go to Your Profile & Complete Setup

After logging in, click on Profile in the top menu. You'll be taken to the profile setup page where you fill in your basic information:

• Username — your public display name on FairQuest
• First & Last Name
• City & State (optional)
• Profile Photo (optional)

🚨 FairQuest is a family-friendly platform. Inappropriate usernames will result in permanent account removal.
5

Click Save & Continue

Once you've filled in your info, click Save & Continue. Your profile is now set up and you can start exploring fairs, saving favorites, and using all FairQuest features.
