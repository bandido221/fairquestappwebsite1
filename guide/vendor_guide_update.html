Updating Your Profile & Fairs

Need to change your vendor info, update your photo, or add and remove fairs? Here's how to do it from your Vendor Dashboard.

← Back to Dashboard Guide 🏰 Applying to a Fair →
UPDATING YOUR PROFILE

How to Update Your Vendor Profile

You can update your vendor information at any time from your Vendor Dashboard. Changes will be submitted for review.

1

Go to Your Vendor Dashboard & Click "Update Profile"

From your Vendor Dashboard, find your application summary and click the Update Profile button. This opens the update form with your current information already filled in.

2

Edit Your Vendor Information

You can update any of the following fields:

• Vendor Name — your business name as it appears on FairQuest.
• Quick Bio — a short description of what you sell (max 50 words).
• Photo — upload a new photo or remove the existing one.
• Vendor Type — Artisan/Craft or Food Vendor.
• Contact Name — the person fair owners should contact.
• Contact Email — where fair owners and FairQuest can reach you.

3

Update Your Photo

If you have an existing photo, you'll see a preview with a Remove Image button. Click it to delete the current photo, then upload a new one. If you have no photo yet, the upload field will be ready to use directly.

💡 Tip: A clear, high-quality photo of your booth or products makes a great first impression with fair owners.
MANAGING YOUR FAIRS

Adding & Removing Fairs

You can add new fairs or remove existing ones from the same update page.

4

Add a New Fair

Scroll down to the Fairs Attending section. Click + Add Another Fair, then search for the fair by name. Select the weekend dates you plan to attend by checking the box next to each date.

💡 Can't find your fair? Leave a comment or question on your application and we'll look into adding it to FairQuest.
5

Remove a Fair

To remove a fair, click the × button on the fair block you want to remove. It will be removed when you save your changes.

🚨 Removing a fair from your profile does not automatically cancel any agreements you have made with that fair. Please contact the fair directly if you need to withdraw.
6

Save Your Changes

Once you've made all your edits, click Save Changes. A confirmation window will appear showing a summary of what was changed. Review it and click Continue to submit, or Cancel to go back and make more edits.

💡 Note: After saving, your changes will be submitted for review. You'll be redirected back to your Vendor Dashboard automatically.
