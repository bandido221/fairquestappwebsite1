🔑 How to Log In and Use Your Profile

📌 Step 1: Go to the Login Page

From the FairQuest homepage, click the "Log In" button.

You can also find it in the menu or user section.

📌 Step 2: Log In with Email or Google

Option 1: Enter your email and password, then click "Sign In".

Option 2: Click "Sign in with Google" to log in faster with your Google account.

📌 Step 3: What Happens After You Log In

After you log in, FairQuest will automatically bring you to your profile page.

You will see a welcome message and your user information.

📌 Step 4: Understanding the Profile Page

On your profile page, you will see:

  • 👤 Your username
  • 📧 Your email
  • 🏆 Your current level
  • ⚔️ Your character class, clan, and alignment (if set)
  • 🗓️ Your join date

📌 Step 5: What You Can Do Next

From your profile page, you can:

  • ⚙️ Go to Settings to change your profile info
  • 🏰 Click Home to start exploring fairs
  • 🚪 Click Log Out if you are done

Everything you need starts from your profile page!
