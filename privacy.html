Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2025

At FairQuest, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information across the FairQuest website and app.

Information We Collect

  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Username and profile information (including optional profile picture)
  • Vendor and performer profile details (if applicable)
  • Fair information submissions (if applicable)
  • Points, tokens, and check-in history
  • Basic usage analytics (such as pages visited, clicks, and device type)

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user profiles, vendor profiles, performer profiles, and fair submissions
  • To track progress, points, and user check-ins
  • To display fairs, vendors, and performers within the app and on the website
  • To send important updates, rewards, or notices to users
  • To improve FairQuest features, security, and experience

Third-Party Services

We use Supabase to securely store and manage your authentication and profile data. Supabase follows strong encryption and security standards. We also use Google Analytics for anonymous visitor analytics.

Camera Use & Permissions

The FairQuest mobile app requests permission to access your device's camera. This is used solely for user-initiated activities such as submitting quest photos or capturing fair moments within the app experience.

All photo interactions are initiated and controlled by the user. No photos are taken without your explicit action, and the app does not perform any facial recognition or biometric processing.

Captured images may be uploaded to secure cloud storage to support in-game activities, but they are never shared with third parties or used for advertising.

You may deny camera access if you do not wish to use photo-related features.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to operate the platform. You can request deletion of your account at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your profile information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of certain optional communications

To exercise your rights, email us at fairquest2025@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as FairQuest grows. Updates will be posted here, and major changes will be announced through the app or website.
