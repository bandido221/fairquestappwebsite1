Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 30, 2025

At FairQuest, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information on the FairQuest website and mobile app (“Services”).

Information We Collect

  • Account & Profile: email address, username, display name, optional profile photo, vendor/performer details (if you choose to create/associate these profiles).
  • App Content You Provide: fair submissions, team/guild information, quest/check-in photos you choose to upload.
  • Location Data (see “Location” section below): approximate and/or precise location with your permission to power nearby fairs, check-ins, and optional local features.
  • Usage & Diagnostics: app events (pages/screens viewed, taps, feature usage), device type, language, basic performance and crash logs.
  • Identifiers: user ID, and mobile push token (e.g., FCM token) used to deliver notifications.
  • Gameplay/Progress: points, tokens, achievements, check-in history, saved/bookmarked fairs.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate core features (accounts, teams, check-ins, fairs, vendors/performers, bookmarks, rewards).
  • Show nearby fairs and verify check-ins (uses location when permitted).
  • Send service notifications (e.g., account updates, team changes, reminders) and optional promotional messages.
  • Maintain safety, integrity, and anti-abuse/fraud controls.
  • Analyze and improve product performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Comply with law and enforce our terms.

Location

What we access: With your permission, the app may access your device’s approximate and/or precise location.

Why: to (a) display nearby fairs and local content, and (b) enable check-in and related features (e.g., validating you’re within a fair’s radius). If you enable optional reminders or automatic check-in, we may evaluate geofence events to trigger those features.

Foreground vs. Background: Location is typically used while you are using the app. If you enable features that require geofences to work reliably, the OS may allow the app to receive location/geofence events when the app is not open. You can turn these features off in-app (if provided) and you can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings.

What we keep: We do not continuously record your path. For check-ins, we may store the fair identifier and geofence center/radius and the fact that a check-in occurred (with timestamp). Raw GPS points are not stored on an ongoing basis for tracking.

Sharing & sale: We do not sell location data. We may share it only with service providers that help us deliver mapping/geofence/analytics and are bound by contracts restricting use to our instructions.

Photos & Camera

Camera access is used only when you choose to capture photos for quests or fair moments. Photos are uploaded to secure storage to support in-app features and are not sold or used for third-party advertising. No biometric or face recognition is performed. You can deny camera access and still use non-photo features.

Push Notifications

We use a device push token to send notifications (e.g., team updates, check-ins, reminders). You can manage or disable notifications in your device settings. Notification content is limited to FairQuest features and updates.

Third-Party Services (Processors)

  • Supabase (authentication, database, file storage, realtime): stores your account/profile data and app content under encryption. Privacy
  • Firebase/Google (push messaging, analytics, crash reporting): delivers notifications and collects usage/performance metrics. Privacy
  • Image & media hosting/CDN (as applicable): serves user-uploaded images and assets efficiently.

These providers act on our behalf and are contractually restricted from using your data for their own purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with: (a) service providers listed above; (b) other users, when you intentionally interact (e.g., team members see team-related info); (c) authorities when required by law; or (d) a successor in a merger/acquisition with appropriate safeguards.

Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

  • Contract (provide the Services you request).
  • Legitimate interests (security, integrity, analytics to improve the Services).
  • Consent (e.g., location permission, notifications where required).

Your Choices & Rights

  • Access and update profile information in-app.
  • Control permissions at any time in device settings (location, camera, notifications).
  • Opt out of non-essential emails or notifications.
  • Request account/data deletion by emailing fairquest2025@gmail.com.

Data Retention

We keep data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services. Typical examples: profile data (while your account exists), check-in records/points history (while needed for rewards and auditing), diagnostics (short periods necessary to improve stability). We delete or de-identify data when no longer needed.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards (encryption in transit/at rest where supported, access controls, and monitoring). No system can be 100% secure; we work to protect your information and continually improve our defenses.

International Transfers

We may process and store information in countries other than where you live. When we transfer data, we use appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable law.

Children’s Privacy

FairQuest is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us to request deletion.

In-App Disclosures & Permissions Summary

  • Location: used for nearby fairs and check-ins; optional background processing may occur if you enable geofence-based features. Manage in device settings.
  • Camera: used only when you capture photos you choose to upload.
  • Notifications: used to deliver account and team updates; manage in device settings.

Contact Us

Questions or requests? Email fairquest2025@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as FairQuest grows. We will post updates here and, if changes are significant, provide additional notice (e.g., in-app).
