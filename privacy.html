Location

Why. (a) Show nearby fairs and local content; (b) verify check-ins (confirm you are within a fair‚Äôs radius); (c) optional geofence reminders/automatic check-in if you turn them on.

Foreground vs. Background. Location is typically used while you use the app. If you enable geofence features, your OS may deliver geofence/location events when the app is not open to make those features work. Background location is off by default and entirely optional.

What we keep. We do not continuously record your path. For check-ins, we may store the fair identifier, geofence center/radius, and the fact that a check-in occurred with timestamp. We do not maintain a continuous GPS trail.

Sharing & sale. We do not sell location data. We share it only with service providers that help deliver mapping, geofence, analytics, or hosting and who are bound by contracts restricting use to our instructions.

Your control. You can deny or revoke location permission any time in device settings, and you can disable geofence features in-app (if provided).
