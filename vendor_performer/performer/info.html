What is FairQuest?

FairQuest is a mobile app and performer platform designed to help Renaissance fairgoers connect with amazing entertainers like you. From musicians to magicians, jesters to jugglers—this is your stage beyond the fairgrounds.

How It Helps Performers

  • Spotlight: Feature your act so audiences can discover you by name, act type, or scheduled performance.
  • Tour Schedule: Let fans know where they can see you perform next.
  • Promotion: Add photos, a bio, and links to your site or socials to grow your fanbase.
  • Connections: Get discovered by organizers, other performers, and future fans alike.
  • Bonus Perks: Early testers get priority listings and exclusive performer rewards!

Why Join?

FairQuest isn’t just a map—it’s your online portfolio, a performer hub, and a way to stay connected with the people who love your craft.

If you bring magic to the stage, we’ll help bring the audience to you.
